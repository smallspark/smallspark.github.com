a Co-working Co-operative

We're a small software company looking to move into our own space, but we can't afford the rent for an entire office ourselves.

To solve this problem, we have decided to look for people that we could share the office (and the cost) with. A nice side effect might be that we create a great co-working space for like-minded people.

The plan is to rent a modestly sized office (60-90m2) in the South Brisbane area, as close to Cultural Centre busway station as possible.

The cost per desk will be around $100/week, which will include 24-hour access to the office, and unlimited wireless internet access.

The maximum number of people in the space will probably be between 9 and 15, depending on the size of the space we find.

If you are interested in getting the details of this arrangement, please enter your email below and we will get in contact with you.
